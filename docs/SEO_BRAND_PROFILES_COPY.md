### Long description (~500–800 caracteres)
```
Pessy is the consumer app for pet owners that brings order to everyday pet care. Tutors upload veterinary documents and Pessy automatically reads vaccines, dates, treatments, and upcoming checkups. The app sends reminders for vaccines, medications, and appointments, generates a shareable digital pet ID, and lets families share pet care between co-tutors.

Available as a PWA (pessy.app) and as native iOS and Android wrappers. Built in Argentina, focused on Latin American Spanish-speaking markets.

Pessy is not a medical app — it does not diagnose or prescribe. It complements veterinarians by keeping everything organized.
```

## Reglas para usar este copy

1. **No agregar claims de salud médica.** Pessy NO diagnostica, NO recomienda tratamientos, NO es una app clínica.
2. **No exagerar números.** Si todavía no tenemos N usuarios, no decir "miles". Decir "early users" o "in beta".
3. **No prometer features que no están en producción.** Vet tipo Uber, tienda integrada — no van todavía.
4. **Idioma:** español rioplatense en perfiles dirigidos a AR/UY, neutral para resto de LATAM, inglés solo para Product Hunt internacional o PH submission.
5. **Tono:** humano, práctico, claro. Nunca técnico. Nunca clínico.
